About

I'm a SOC Analyst based in Maryland with a B.S. in Information Technology from Towson University. I monitor and investigate security alerts across managed client environments — triaging threats from Microsoft Sentinel and Huntress EDR, auditing identities in Active Directory and Microsoft Entra ID, and tuning detections to cut false positives and lower MTTR.

Before security, I spent years in IT and analytics — building GPOs and rolling out MFA at an enterprise scale, and writing SQL & Python ETL pipelines and Tableau dashboards as a Data Analyst Captain. That blend is my edge: I read logs like datasets and communicate findings clearly to technical and non-technical stakeholders alike.
